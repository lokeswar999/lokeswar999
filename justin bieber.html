Justin Bieber

Justin Drew Bieber is a Canadian pop musician and singer-songwriter. Justin was discovered in 2008 by Scooter Braun, who came across his videos on YouTube and later became his manager. Scooter arranged for him to meet with Usher in Atlanta, and Bieber was signed to Raymond Braun Media Group (RBMG), and then to an Island Records (Def Jam Island) recording contract offered by record executive L.A. Reid. Justin has released 6 studio albums, 3 Compilation albums, 2 EPs and 3 Remix albums. Justin's fans are called Beliebers. Justin has 7 albums that have debuted at number 1 on the Billboard 200 chart and Justin Bieber has broken a US chart record set by Elvis Presley 59 years ago, by becoming the youngest artist to score seven No 1 albums. Elvis's estate tweeted its congratulations on to Justin on the chart achievement of 21st Century King of Pop
